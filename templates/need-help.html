Need help?

  1. Use this if you have visual impairments (e.g. colourblindness),
    or only have mobile device (not recommended),
    or just need help with any of the stages,
    or if you consistently encounter an error on our webpage.
  2. You may email saw.zedong@dhs.sg.
  3. Please also introduce your name and class.
  4. Please also try to avoid spamming the email unnecessarily.
  5. If your email is sent after 19 January, 8pm,
    you may not get a response in time to complete it before the deadline.
  6. Thank you!
